About

I design learning grounded in psychology.

Indah Setiawan
Learning Designer

I'm a Jakarta-based learning designer who believes that spending more time on the reasoning behind a lesson matters more than the slide count. Over the last four years, I've shipped 300+ learning assets — video scripts, interactive H5P modules, facilitator guides, and assessments — for both corporate and educational programs.

My approach blends ADDIE and Backward Design with insights from cognitive psychology on how people learn, then tests this thinking against real-world constraints, such as tight timelines, LMS platforms with limited features, and learners who would rather be doing anything else.
